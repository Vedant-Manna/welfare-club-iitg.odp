About

The Welfare Club at IIT Guwahati aims to promote holistic growth and welfare among the student community. Through various initiatives, workshops, and drives, the club ensures the well-being of all its members. Our mission is to foster a supportive and inclusive environment where students thrive academically, socially, and emotionally, paving the way for future leaders and innovators.
